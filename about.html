Our Journey of Excellence

Founded by S. Sohanlal Dhiman in 1990, Dhiman Welding Works has grown from a small manufacturing unit to a leading name in the industrial equipment sector. With over 35 years of experience, we have successfully installed more than 100 systems across India.

Our commitment to quality and innovation has made us the most appreciated and applauded manufacturers of Air Pollution Control Devices. We believe that "what is produced is not more important than how it is produced" - a philosophy that drives our entire manufacturing process.
